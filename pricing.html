What services does APlus Drivers offer?

We specialize in professional driver outsourcing and transportation services for individuals, families, and businesses. Our offerings include personal chauffeurs, corporate driver placements, school runs, event drivers, and more.

How quickly can I get a driver?

You can get a driver within 30 minutes, depending on your location and availability.

Are your drivers background-checked and trained?

Yes. All our drivers go through thorough background checks, driving assessments, and customer service training to ensure your safety and comfort.

Can I request the same driver every time?

Unfortunately, we do not currently guarantee the same driver for each booking. However, we ensure that every assigned driver meets our high standards of professionalism and reliability.

Do you provide cars as well, or just drivers?

Yes, we also offer car rental services. Available vehicles include Toyota Corolla, Prado, G-Wagon, Hilux, Hiace Bus, and Coaster Bus, depending on your needs and budget.

What areas do you serve?

We operate across Lagos and most other parts of Nigeria, except designated red zones such as Imo State and certain northern regions (excluding Abuja).

How do I book a driver?

Booking is easy. Simply send us a direct message (DM) on our social media channels or call our customer support line.

What happens if my assigned driver is unavailable?

We will assign a replacement driver immediately to avoid any delays in your schedule.

How are your prices determined?

Pricing depends on the type of service, location, and duration. Within Lagos, our rates start at a minimum of N20,000 per day.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Yes. You can reschedule once if the driver has not yet arrived at your location. For cancellations, please notify us as early as possible.

What makes APlus Drivers different from other driver services?

We combine speed, professionalism, safety, and personalized service. Our drivers are well-groomed, courteous, and trained to handle a wide range of client needs with discretion and care.

Do you offer long-term driver contracts for companies?

Yes, we provide long-term driver placements for businesses and corporate organizations. Custom packages are available.

Can I get a driver for school runs or family errands?

Yes. We provide responsible drivers for school pick-ups, drop-offs, and everyday family errands.

Do you offer services on weekends and public holidays?

Yes. We provide responsible drivers for school pick-ups, drop-offs, and everyday family errands.

How do you handle complaints or service issues?

We have a 24/7 customer support desk ready to address any concerns or feedback you may have.

Can I interview or test the driver before hiring?

Yes. You may interview or test the driver to ensure they’re a good fit for your specific needs.

Do your drivers wear uniforms?

Yes. Our drivers are dressed in clean, corporate attire to maintain a professional appearance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept payments via bank transfer.

Is there customer support if I need help during my booking?

Yes. Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you before, during, and after your booking.
